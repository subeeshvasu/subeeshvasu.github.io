I am a Post-doctoral Researcher in the Computer Vision Lab, EPFL, Switzerland. I work on various topics on computer vision with Prof. Pascal Fua. My current research topics include image segmentation, medical image processing, and 3D shape modeling. Before joining EPFL, I was a Ph.D. student at the Image Processing and Computer Vision Lab, Dept. of Electrical Engineering, IIT madras. I work in the areas of image processing and computer vision with Prof. A. N. Rajagopalan. My PhD research topics includes image restoration, scene inference, underwater imaging, and deep learning. Before joining IIT madras, I graduated from GECI with B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering and M.Tech in Signal processing from NIT Calicut.
